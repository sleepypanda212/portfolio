Hello there!

I am an undergraduate studying Computer Science and Biology at Brown University who loves digital illustration and art. I believe that the most accessible and useable interfaces look good as well. To achieve this, I design and digitally illustrate many parts of the interfaces myself in addition to testing and experimenting different interfaces that are easy to navigate.

My inspiration for UIUX designs come from my love for animation and fashion, which I try to weave into my projects. Outside of UIUX, I also use R and python to study cancer progression using RNA-seq data at a lab at Brown.

When I'm not coding or doing research, you can find me playing golf, watching Studio Ghibli movies, walking inside art museums, and playing with my dog (she's the logo of my portfolio in the top left corner :D).
