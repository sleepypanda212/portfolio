Project highlights

Rever
Rever is a start-up aimiing to make returns easier by consolidating returns into one location and allowing consumers to return their products in 2 clicks. Wthout looking at Rever's interface, I designed a mobile app for Rever, primarily focusing on usability. My three colleagues and I brainstormed an interface that is the most intuitive for users. We received feedbacks from peers and industry experts and analyzed the user testing results to iteratively design an interface that best suits Rever.
Beary Cute Shop
I designed an online shopping website for Beary Cute Shop that sells various clothing types, accessories, and footwears. This interface explores various filtering and sorting options to enable an easier and a more convenient online shopping experience regardless of the wide range of items. In addition, I integrated digital illustration into the interface to enhance the shopping experience and boost customer satisfaction.
Metro Nail
Metro Nail is a small nail salon in Providence. I redesigned the website for Metro Nail to improve a pre-existng interface based on useability and accessibility that are also responsive. On top of analyzing and resolving accessibility and useability issues, I designed an interface optimized for different media (phone, tablet, and computer screen sizes).
Gas Stove
Knowing the target audience of the interface is crucial, as even an everyday appliance like a gas stove can have a different meaning and nuiance to different users depending on the familiarity with the appliance as well as the environment the user is in. I tested this "personalization of user experience" through experiments and constructed personas and a storyboard based on the persona.
